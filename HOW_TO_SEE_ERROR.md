# Quick Guide: How to See the Error Message

## Simple Steps:

1. **Go to**: https://github.com/nitish-yaddala/portfolio-website/actions

2. **Click** on the most recent failed run (red X icon)

3. **Scroll down** to see the jobs section

4. **Click** on the "build" job (it should be red/failed)

5. **Look for** the step that failed - it will have a red X next to it

6. **Click** on that step name (like "Build" or "Install dependencies")

7. **Scroll through** the output - the error will be near the bottom

8. **Copy the error message** - it usually starts with something like:
   - "Error: ..."
   - "Failed to ..."
   - "Type error: ..."
   - "Cannot find module ..."

## Or Take a Screenshot:

Just take a screenshot of the failed step output and share it!
